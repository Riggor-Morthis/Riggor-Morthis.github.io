Pacific Defenders

Can you make a "professional" board game in a week ?... No... Sort of... It's complicated. The goal of this project was to create a board game with "professional" standards (custom pieces, edited and printed rule book...) in one week and on my own. Turns out that creating physical pawns and the board to go with them is very time-consuming, and that it's very easy to forget to print your rules when the deadline is near.

But, after the testing phase, I can say that it' i's possible to create a fun and somewhat original game (don't pay attention to the fact that I stole Pacific Rim's universe and story) in a week. I will soon publish a new and improved version of this game, accompanied by an article (in English for once) which will detail my experience and the lessons I learned.
