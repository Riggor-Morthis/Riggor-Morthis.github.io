Palette Creator

A short project made using Python, mainly to get out of my comfort zone. The video is relatively concise, but I'll summarise this software nonetheless: it's a palette creator, in which you specify your main colour and the kind of palette you want, and which will then automatically pick all the other colours.

Although I still use it sometimes, I am not very satisfied with the final product. I chose Python mainly to think outside my box, but if I had to do it again, I would use better framework, as well as C++.
