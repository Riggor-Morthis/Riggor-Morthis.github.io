Tiny D10

As a long time fan of role-playing games (especially as the GM), and of rule-writing, I have recently been working on the TinyD10 system. Based on the rules of the same name by Aaron Carlson, it is a translation of the rules, from English to French, as well as expansion of the base content. I chose this ruleset because it was both free (easy to share with my players) and simple (perfect for beginners), while being easy to expand and add onto.

From a gameplay point of view, it is a lightweight and technically genre-agnostic (or at least easily adaptable) ruleset, while still being pretty basics. During my translation, the rules' website disappeared from the internet, which allowed me to create my own content and integrate it directly into the rules as they were being written. For example, they are 4 new classes and 4 new races, and many of the rules (especially those about combat) have been improved and expanded upon. Even though this system is currently in use in 2 different campaigns, I am not satisfied with it, and, as with many of my projects, a V2 is currently being written.
