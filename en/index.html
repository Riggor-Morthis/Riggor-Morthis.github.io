Sylvain Rocland, former student in computer science and recent graduate (master "Imagine" at University of Montpellier).
I am someone curious and adaptable, who was regularly complimented on my autonomy and my rigor.

As a lifelong video game fan, I pursued a 5-year degree in computer science to be a part of the industry. During those years I got a taste for the process, from the initial ideas to the final tests.
Of a creative nature, I am passionate about role-playing games (especially as a rule writer and a game master), but also by car mechanics, which satisfies my taste for problem solving.
