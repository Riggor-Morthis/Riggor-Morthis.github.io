The follow-up to the two previous projects. I'm rather satisfied with my gameplay loop and the basic principle of DDD (how you're aware of the monsters' next move and you must plan around it), but my previous iterations didn't go too deeply into it since they were made in 5 days. So, I started working on this project again, now that time is not as much a factor.
You're still a medieval fantasy hero, living in a kingdom invaded by the Darkkness. You'll to go into sinister and malevolant dungeons to defeat the monsters and free your homeland.
