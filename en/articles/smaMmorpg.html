A university project on the theme of AI, made in Unity. My goal was to emulate an MMORPG environment in which players are given quests to level up, and communicate with each other to maximise their experience gain while minimising their risks (they only have one life). These AIs work according to the BDI principle: they know (or think they know) their immediate environment and their quest's informations, have desires (staying alive, leveling up quickly...), and analyse these two elements to find the best course of action. Players are able to work in pairs, by "talking" to another player in the hope that they join the quest we sent them.
Above their heads, each player shows his life points, his current behaviour, his current quest and level, and show their group by their shirt colour.
