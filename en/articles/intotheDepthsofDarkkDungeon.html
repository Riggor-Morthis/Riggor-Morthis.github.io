Created while I was searching for an internship to end my studies, it's a technical test for a company where the assignment was to create a mobile game in 5 days, then deliver it to them. My game is made in Unity, and is in 2D since it is the graphic style I know best, and therefore the one I am able to produce the fastest. I tried to have a very arcadey and simple gameplay to fit the mobile theme, while still asking a little bit of strategic thinking from the players.
In this game, you play as a knight whose goal is to go through small levels by slaying skeletons on your way. The first levels were created by hand, to act as a tutorial, but the next ones are procedurally generated to give infinite content to the players.
